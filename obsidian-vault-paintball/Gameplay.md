# Initial Demo

The initial demo for this game is supposed to feature only a duel mode (between two players across a network)


## Player

- First-person view only

## Possible actions

- Move: 4-direction movement
- Turn view: Move the mouse to rotate the player's head
- Shoot
- Dash: Pressing the dash button makes the player gain a short burst of speed in the last direction pressed
- Slide: Gain a short boost of speed in a single direction; Player view goes into a lower position
